About Inside Insight

To Build Your Best Career

We are Inside Insight, a website created by five young professionals to increase the accessibility of quality career advice. We keep our five values at the front and center of everything we do. We always try to be:

Helpful

Transparent

Our content is meant to help young tech workers and aspiring students make informed career decisions by providing the insights of others in the field. Helping others is central to our mission.

We hope to demystify the tech world so that we can help our audience navigate their careers with confidence.

Authentic

Always Relevant

We provide access to real people and their real experience so that you can know what working in the field is really like.

The world, and especially the tech industry, is constantly changing. We ensure that we provide up to date and relevant information for the next generation of tech workers to succeed at their careers

Review Policy

Product and service reviews are an important part of our content and business. When we write reviews, we know that it is important to keep our five values front and center. This means that we only write reviews on tools and services that we think may, in some way, help our readers in their careers. We know this is key to staying helpful and relevant. We also take efforts to stay current, and won’t show any reviews that haven’t been updated in the last year.

Finally, we promise that our reviews are not influenced by our advertisers and business partners, and that we receive no direct compensation from our advertisers and business partners for the publication of our reviews.
